About SmartSpend

Your money,
finally under control

SmartSpend is a personal expense management system built to help individuals and families take full control of their financial lives. Whether you're saving for a goal, trying to cut back on spending, or just want to know where your money goes each month — SmartSpend has you covered.

We built SmartSpend on the belief that financial clarity shouldn't require a finance degree. Simple inputs, smart insights, and clear visuals — that's the SmartSpend promise.

Features

Everything you need to
manage your money

From tracking a coffee to planning a mortgage — SmartSpend handles it all in one clean interface.

💳

Transaction Tracking

Log every income and expense with amount, category, date and notes. Filter and search across your full history instantly.

🏷️

Smart Categories

12 predefined categories — Food, Transport, Rent, Entertainment and more. Create your own custom categories with emoji and color.

🎯

Monthly Budgets

Set spending limits per category for any month. Visual progress bars show exactly how much you've used and what's left.

📈

Charts & Reports

Bar charts for income vs expenses over 6 months. Doughnut charts for category breakdown. Weekly spending trends.

🚨

Overspend Alerts

Automatic warnings when you hit 80% of a budget. Red alerts when you exceed 100%. Stay ahead of your limits.

🤖

Auto-Categorization

Type "Netflix" and SmartSpend picks Entertainment. Type "Uber" and it picks Transport. Rule-based keyword matching saves you time.

📄

PDF & CSV Export

Generate professional monthly reports as PDF or raw data as CSV. Perfect for tax time or sharing with an accountant.

🔌

REST API

Full JSON API for transactions and summaries. Integrate SmartSpend data with your own tools or mobile apps.

🔒

Secure & Private

Each user sees only their own data. Django's battle-tested auth system with password hashing and CSRF protection.
